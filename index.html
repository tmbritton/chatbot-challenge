The Autoboss Chatbot Turing Test Challenge

What?

A challenge to see if you can make a chatbot that fools your coworkers into thinking it's JC.

Why?

Most interest when asking for suggestions was about chatbots.

It's not a big step from making a silly chatbot to a useful one.

How?

Hipchat will be our interface.

We'll put JC and chatbots in a chatroom and ask questions.

Audience will vote on which one is the real JC.

When?

We'll present results next quarter's Innovation Time.
